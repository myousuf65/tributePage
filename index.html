Dr Israr Ahmed

The Intellectual Icon of 21st Century

Potrait of Dr Israr Ahmed while giving Darse Quran

Here is a timeline of Dr Israr Ahmed's Life :

  • 26 April 1932 - Born in Hisar, Punjab
  • Post 1947 - briefly worked for Muslim Student's Federation
  • 1950 - he joined Jamaat-e-Islami led by Abul Ala Maududi and moved to Lahore to attend the King Edward Medical University in 1950
  • 1957 - left Jamaat-e-Islami when the latter opted for participating in electoral politics
  • 1954 - received his MBBS degree from King Edward Medical University and began practising medicine
  • 1956 - founded 1956 founded Tanzeem-e-Islami, an attempt to create a "disciplined organization"
  • 1965 - obtained his master's degree in Islamic Studies from the University of Karachi
  • 2010 - died at age of 77 years old
